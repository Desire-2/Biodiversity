Our Projects

Restoration for Degraded Area on Karago Lake

We are working tirelessly to restore and conserve the degraded area around Karago Lake. Our efforts aim to revitalize the ecosystem, protect wildlife, and provide a sustainable environment for future generations.

Monitoring

Our monitoring initiatives play a crucial role in assessing and safeguarding the biodiversity in our region. We use data-driven approaches to track changes in wildlife populations and their habitats.

Community Education and Afforestation on the Nyiarakigugu Lake Edge

We engage with local communities to raise awareness about conservation and actively involve them in afforestation efforts along the Nyiarakigugu Lake edge. Together, we are creating a greener, healthier future.

Making Native Trees Nursery

Native trees are the backbone of our ecosystem. We have established a nursery dedicated to growing native trees, ensuring a sustainable source for reforestation and restoration projects.
