Mission

    • Our mission is to empower youth and communities through meaningful conservation activities that cultivate a deep connection to nature and a dedication to preserving the country's rich biodiversity.
    • We extend our mission to professional courses, guiding individuals to become superheroes in conservation through expert courses and training.
    • Additionally, we facilitate community engagement, helping students and graduates connect with industry sectors related to their studies and apply their knowledge in practical, real-world contexts.

Vision

Our vision is to see a Rwanda where biodiversity thrives, wildlife is protected, and future generations are dedicated stewards of the environment, ensuring a sustainable and harmonious coexistence between people and nature.
We aspire to a future where individuals are not only passionate about conservation but also possess the analytical skills to make informed decisions and contribute to data-driven insights, bridging the gap between academia and industry.
